Norman (1066-1154)

1066-1154
St Carantoc Church, Crantock

The church of St Carantoc is located in the village of Crantock.

St Symphorian's Church, Forrabury

This is the parish church of Forrabury, which is also one of the churches serving the nearby market town of Boscastle.

St Genesius's Church, St Gennys

The Church of St Genesius lies near to some attractive Cornish coastline. It is in the Early English style.

St Winwallo's Church, Tremaine

A tiny parish church on a hill.

The Monastery of St Michael's Mount

This is one of the most distinctive and spectacular spots in Cornwall.

St Keira's Church, Egloskerry

The church of St Keira is located in the middle of the village of Egloskerry.

St Germanus Parish Church, St Germans

The Church of St Germans is essentially within a village inextricably related to the Port Eliot estate (whose house is located next to the church), all situated close to the river and in a generously wooded landscape.

St Brueredus Parish Church, St Breward

The parish church of St Bureredus serves the village of St Breward, which is located in a lonely spot on the edge of Bodmin Moor.
